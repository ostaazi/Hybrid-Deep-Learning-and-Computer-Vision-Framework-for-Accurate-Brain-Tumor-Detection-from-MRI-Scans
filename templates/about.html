What We Detect

Our AI system is trained to identify and classify four main categories of brain conditions from MRI scans.

Glioma

Tumors that originate in the glial cells of the brain, requiring immediate medical attention.

Meningioma

Typically benign tumors that develop in the meninges, the protective layers around the brain.

Pituitary

Tumors affecting the pituitary gland, which can impact hormone production and various bodily functions.

No Tumor

Normal brain tissue with no detectable abnormalities or tumor presence in the MRI scan.
